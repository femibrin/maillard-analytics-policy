Maillard Analytics

Effective date: July 11, 2026 · Contact: femi.brinson@gmail.com

Privacy Policy · Terms of Service

Maillard Analytics is a private, single-creator analytics tool. It retrieves social media content metadata and performance statistics for one creator's own accounts (TikTok, Instagram), with that creator's explicit authorization, and presents them in a private dashboard and periodic reports used by the creator and her authorized business operator. It is not a public product and has no other users.

Privacy Policy

What we collect

With the account owner's explicit OAuth authorization, the tool retrieves: basic profile information (display name, avatar, follower counts) and content-level metadata and statistics (video/post titles, captions, URLs, view counts, like counts, comment counts, share counts, and similar performance metrics) from the TikTok Display API and Instagram (Meta) Graph API. Publicly available content metadata from public profiles may also be collected for benchmarking.

What we do with it

Data is stored in a private, access-controlled database and used solely to produce analytics and strategy reports for the creator whose accounts are connected. It is never sold, rented, shared with third parties, used for advertising, or used to train machine-learning models.

What we do not collect

No private messages, no contact lists, no payment information, no data about the creator's followers as individuals, and no data from any account that has not granted authorization.

Data retention and deletion

Data is retained while the tool is in active use. The account owner may revoke access at any time via their TikTok or Instagram security settings, and may request full deletion of stored data by emailing the contact address above; deletion is completed within 30 days.

Third-party services

Data is processed and stored using Supabase (database hosting) and Google Cloud (content classification). These providers act as processors only.
